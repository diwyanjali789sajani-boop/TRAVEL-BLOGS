Travel Stories

Here are a few of my favorite destinations and memories from my journeys.

Ella, Sri Lanka

Nestled in Sri Lanka’s hill country, Ella is known for its breathtaking views, cool climate, and tea plantations.

  • Hike to Little Adam’s Peak
  • Walk across Nine Arches Bridge
  • Explore tea plantations

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress surrounded by gardens and history.

  • Climb the Lion Rock fortress
  • Visit ancient frescoes
  • Explore water gardens

Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka

Known as “Little England,” Nuwara Eliya offers a cool escape with its colonial charm, tea estates, and gardens.

  • Gregory Lake boat rides
  • Hakgala Botanical Garden
  • Tea factory tours
